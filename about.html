Our Origins

Arabica
A high-quality coffee bean known for its smooth, slightly sweet flavor and aromatic complexity. It thrives in high-altitude regions.
Robusta
A stronger, more bitter coffee bean with higher caffeine content. Often used in espresso blends for a richer crema.
Fair Trade
A certification system that ensures farmers are paid fairly, work in safe conditions, and follow sustainable farming practices.
